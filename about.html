About Me

I was born on the 23rd October 2002 in the small town of Novomoskovsk, Ukraine. At the age of 6, I went to school, where I attended additional classes of choreography and drawing. A year after I entered to children circus studio called “Melange-art” in Novomoskovsk. In 2016, being a part of a group of neon jugglers I became the finalist of the show called “Ukraine’s got talent. Kids". From 2017 to 2018 I worked as a juggler and a ballet dancer in famous Ukrainian Circus called “Kobzov”. In 2018 I entered the Kiev Municipal Academy of Circus and Performing Arts. Then I graduated from Junior Specialist and entered the Bachelor's degree in 2021. I study juggling and contortion with hoops, as well as acting, pantomime, classical and modern choreography . Since there my adventure has begun.
